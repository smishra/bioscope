Logical Expression
-Any combination using ‘and’ , ‘or’, ‘not’ and
a special case where combination could be
expressed. For. e.g
(2A and 2B) or (2A and 2E) – at least two of A
along with two of either B or E
2(ABC) – two of any combinarion of A or B or C
for e.g AA, AB, AC, BB, BC, CC are valid
